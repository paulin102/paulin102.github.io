Page description

This is my collection of the best Crowley/Aziraphale stories chosen by me. It contains long stories, of which some are unfinished, alternative universe stories and short stories, that concentrates on one arc.

Some of them are better written, but all of them gave me a lot of joy to read and are worth to remember.

On main page can be unarranged stories, that will find their place in proper subpages.

Amazing source of knowledge about series:

Be Still My Soul

3-part story placed in Finland during World War I.

The Scribe's Tale

Aziraphale is a monk and Crowley is a knight in the Middle Age.

That This Could Be The kingdom

Aziraphale is a priest and he met Crowley in the seminar. Crowley resigned and they didn't see each other for many years. They meet again at the debate and try to be friends again.

Azira Fell And The Apocalypse Scroll

Egypt, 1935. Aziraphale is an archeologist and looking for an ancient scroll. Crowley is supposed to be his guide, but he also works for people trying to get to the scroll before Aziraphale.

Only Love (Can Bring the Rain)

Aziraphale is a pronce and Crowley is a gardener. They become friends since childhood, but their feelings change when they get older. At the same time Gabriel, Aziraphale's brother tries to marry him to some nobleman.

Such Rebel Blood

Aziraphale proved that Crowley is innocent of murder and couple months later they meet on the murder-mystery game that becomes real.

Borrowed Words

Aziraphale and Crowley were in love in their youth. Years later Crowley is famous author who needs a break. He goes out of town where he finds Aziraphale. It turns out that neither of them is over each other.

Simmer (The Long-Awaited Reply to a Lingering Kiss)

Aziraphale and Crowley were in culinary school together. Across the years they meet in different restaurants in various countires.

Temple of the Muses

Crowley is an escort. Gabriel buys Crowley for month for Aziraphale, but he doesn't want to use Crowley. He asks him for lessons of etiquete instead.

Summer's End

2095, Aziraphale is a prostitue and lives in his caravan. Crowley meets him during his trip and offers him to take care of garden for a place to live.

If The Fates Allow

Crowley and Aziraphale are at the christmas parties for their offices that are held in the same building. They see each other when they go outside. It turns out they met before and they both remember each other.

The Pocket Watch

Crowley works for jewelry thieves and spends a lot of time in Aziraphale's shop. When Aziraphale's shop is robbed he gets his father pocket watch, but doesn't know how to get it back without revealing who he is.

Lunacy

Set in future. Aziraphale and Crowley are on planet in space. When it's time to go back something is very wrong and they need to save their lives.

Protect and Serve

On the surface Aziraphale is a policeman and Crowley a known criminal, but are married and work together to get justice.

Part of the Whole Design

Crowley and Aziraphale met in Paris as students and years later they find each other and realize that their families hates each other.

Work of Heart

Aziraphale volunteers in a library and Crowley joins his library and reads horror stories to children.

All's Fair in Love, War, and Show Business

Aziraphale and Crowley are actors who had fall out years before. Now they need to cooperate to save their careers.

Snake Eyes

Aziraphale and Crowley met when they were kids. During the years they grow really close even though they live apart and their feelings change.

In The Shadows Of Our Past, A Flicker

Crowley and Aziraphale are agents and they go on vacation together to spy on their mark. In order to be unsuspicius they pretend they are married.

Love's Alibi

Aziraphale and Crowley in "When Harry Met Sally".

Like A Feather Flying High

Crowley and Aziraphale are cowboys and it looks like Crowley doesn't like Aziraphale. It changes when they go alone to catch the calf.

The Serpent's House

Crowley runs the orphanage for special kids and Aziraphale is autiting his work.

Remember and Repeat

Set in 1960. Crowley is a new teacher in boarding school and Aziraphale is the only one who likes his methods.

Whatever a Moon Has Meant; Whatever a Sun Will Sing

Aziraphale and Crowley met at school, but later Crowley moves to Germany. When World War I starts they fight against each other.

Tenderfoot

Crowley gets to another planet by cheating. He is not ready for the conditions on the planet and Aziraphale, a guard helps him adapt.

Imperfect by Nature

Aziraphale and Crowley knew each other as children. Some years later Crowley is looking for a flat and rents from Aziraphale, but it turns out he is very annoying neighbour.

A Dependence on Appearance

Crowley hosts a party for young artists and ric patrons to help Anathema. There he meets Aziraphale who is more interested in Crowley than in art.

District of (un-)Certainty

Aziraphale is a PhD student and Crowley works for politician campaign. They rent an apartment in Washington together in order to afford it.

And Salt The Earth Behind You

Aziraphale is a profiler and Crowley is his informant. Even though they love each other they can't be together. To make things worse Aziraphale works on a case of serial killer.

Smooth As Tennessee Whisky

Crowley travels from farm to farm to work. One day he starts working on Fell's farm where he meets owner's son Aziraphale.

Every Day Is a Holiday (when I'm near to you)

Seal of Approval

The Apple of My Eye (But the Garden of Eden Kind)

Anthony of Arcadia

Reap What You Sow

Aziraphale and Crowley met in law school and hit off immediately. Unfortunately Aziraphale disappeared and they don't see each other for years. When they finally meet again Aziraphale is a wedding planner and Crowley divorce lawyer.

Runaway Groom

From Eden

Aziraphale and Crowley were in love as young man and Aziraphale was send away by his family. When he comes back he is a minister of church and waites for his parish. He meets Crowley again and old feelings come back. He also receives letters from secret admirer.

Abyssal

Aziraphale and Crowley are researchers in the bottom of the ocean. When weird things start to happen and rest of the crew leaves the submarine they stay alone.

The Taste That Your Lips Allow

Crowley is a vampire and Aziraphale is a witch, but doesn't know about his powers. They accidentaly meet and fall in love immediately.

Actuarial Risk

Donor Relations

Aziraphale works for fundraiser company. Crowley wants to use it to improve his and his company image. Because of that he spends a lot of time with Aziraphale to discusse all the details.

Minor Chords In A Major Key

Aziraphale is a new student in a boarding school and his roommate is Crowley. Crowley loves playing guitar and wants to be musician in the future, but his father is against it.

Scarlet Hood, Heart of Gold

The Red Hood story in which Crowley is a werewolf and Aziraphale wants to help him.

Common People

Aziraphale is from rich family and wants to learn about normal life, because his family soon will stop support him. He meets Crowley who shows him life.

Digestif

Crowley becomes an heir and gains a lot of money and titles. He doesn't know how to act in society and follows the lead of new friend Aziraphale.

Mid Tempo

Crowley and Aziraphale broke up, but they still work together. One day Crowley proposes they start sexual relationship again, because they're both lonely.

I'd Be Home With You

The Price of Worship
